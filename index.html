About WIE Tech Forum

IEEE WIE Tech Forum 2016 is the first ever female oriented technology forum in Sri Lanka which will be organized by the IEEE WIE Sri Lanka Section. It will be held in Colombo on the 30th of April 2016 with the participation of around 150+ female university students, young innovators, experts, and entrepreneurs.

We aim to,

  • Increase technology awareness in the female community and assist in bridging the digital divide.
  • Provide networking opportunities to female technological innovators and entrepreneurs in the country with the valued sponsorship of the industry.
  • Creates a common platform for the female technological community and the technological industry to network, share ideas, provide career guidances and also to get advices related to innovative technological solutions for the 21st century.

Tech Forum will consists of panel discussions, technological presentations and leadership and personality development sessions which will expand the leadership skills of female tech-enthusiasts which will also develop their personalities. It is our pleasure to provide the female technocrats with the skills they should possess in order to become the STEM leaders in the 21st century.

IEEE WIE Tech Forum brings in leading female role models and professionals from a wide range of industries in the country to a single platform. You can certainly look forward for new technological opportunities.
